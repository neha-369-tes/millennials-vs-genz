Millennials vs Gen Z
Presented by: Speaker 1

Millennials Meme

Gen Z Meme

Millennials

  • Pioneered social media platforms like Facebook
  • Faced 2008 recession, shaping frugality
  • Value work-life balance but often overwork
  • Nostalgic for 90s pop culture
  • Adopted early smartphones with enthusiasm

Gen Z

  • Grew up with TikTok and short-form content
  • Prioritizes mental health and authenticity
  • Entrepreneurial with side hustles
  • Revives Y2K fashion trends
  • Digital natives with instant tech mastery
