Millennials vs Gen Z 😂

The Ultimate Generational Showdown - Who's More Relatable? 🤔

Speaker: Neha

Millennials 😭

"I'm not crying, you're crying"

  • Born gadget-free, raised with the moonlight dinner experience.
    "Nila soru life laam different level da."
    For those who don't know - Nila Soru = eating dinner while watching the moon.
  • Mental health = silent suffering.
    "Naaku stress irundhaa, I'll just sleep it off da."
  • ₹15 filter coffee in a steel tumbler = peak satisfaction.
    "Coffee-na kaapi dhan da."
  • Multitasking pros - attending Zoom calls while making chutney.
    "Client call on one hand, dosa flip on other."
  • Nights = phone calls with crush or crying over exams.
    "Millennials laam night full-a kana muzhi illa BF/GF kita pesuvanga."
  • Lunchbox fights were real.
    "One keerai vitta biryani eduthukaraan da!" Special dish from home? Warzone in class.
  • When BSNL came: conversations began with "Hello? Hello?" and ended with 10 missed calls just to say "Saptacha?"

Gen Z 😎

"I'm built different"

  • Born with phone + Wi-Fi in hand.
  • Grew up watching Shinchan, Doraemon…. and honestly, still watching.
    "Adhu vera kadha da!"
  • Mental health is top priority - journaling, therapy, mood tracking, everything.
  • Cold brew, matcha latte, bubble tea… ₹250 for 3 sips and a vibe.
  • Online class experts - camera off, mic mute, Ludo King ON.
    "Nangala online class la 10th pass pannum da!"
  • Nights = watching sad reels, stalking ex's stories.
    "Pasanga night-la okandhu BF/GF reel pakkaranga da!"
  • All food delivery apps ready - Blinkit, Zomato, one click = biryani at doorstep.
    "Call panna delivery dha da!"
  • Conversations are quick and minimalist:
    "Sup?" "Bored." "Me too." "Seri, reels paakalam da."
